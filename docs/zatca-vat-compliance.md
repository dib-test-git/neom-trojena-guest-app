# ZATCA / VAT compliance notes

Trojena operates inside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and is therefore subject
to **ZATCA** (Zakat, Tax and Customs Authority) Phase 2 e-invoicing rules.
Every guest checkout in this app produces a compliant tax invoice.

## Rates

- Standard VAT rate: **15%** (Article 47, KSA VAT Implementing Regulations).
- Applied to every taxable supply: slopes passes, dining, spa, retail.

## Invoice requirements (Phase 2)

Each invoice generated by `services/reservations` MUST include:

1. Seller details (Trojena Resorts — VAT registration number, address).
2. Buyer details if the guest is a registered business (B2B path).
3. Invoice issuance timestamp (UTC, ISO-8601).
4. Unique invoice identifier (UUID v4 + sequential serial within day).
5. Itemised lines with SKU, description (AR + EN), quantity, unit price.
6. VAT amount per line and totals (subtotal, VAT, gross).
7. **Cryptographic stamp** + **QR code** (TLV-encoded) per ZATCA spec.
8. Previous invoice hash (PIH) chain for tamper-evidence.

## Currency & rounding

- All monetary fields stored as integer **halalas** (`SAR * 100`) to avoid
  floating-point drift. See `services/reservations/src/cart.ts`.
- Rounding: half-up to the nearest halala on VAT totals.

## Integration

- Invoices are POSTed to the ZATCA Fatoora portal staging endpoint in
  pre-production and to the production endpoint in `prod`.
- The clearance response (signed XML + UUID) is persisted alongside the
  booking record.
